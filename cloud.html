Welcome to Indian Tradition

India is a land of diverse cultures and traditions. Its rich heritage reflects in its festivals, rituals, art, and cuisine.

Diwali Festival

Diwali, also known as the festival of lights, symbolizes the victory of light over darkness, good over evil. It is celebrated with lighting diyas, bursting firecrackers, exchanging sweets, and offering prayers.

"Asato ma sad gamaya, Tamaso ma jyotir gamaya, Mrityor ma amritam gamaya." - Lead me from ignorance to truth, from darkness to light, from death to immortality.

Rangoli

Rangoli is a traditional Indian art form where colorful patterns are created on the floor using colored rice, dry flour, colored sand, or flower petals. It is believed to bring good luck and prosperity.
